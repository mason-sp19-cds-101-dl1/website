Instructions

This module exercise is a series of questions posted on Blackboard that check your understanding of the material contained in the week 1 lecture videos. You can go directly to the module exercise on Blackboard by clicking here. This should be completed after you have finished watching the lecture videos.

There is no time limit and you may attempt the module exercises up to three times. Your grade will be the highest score you receive across your attempts.
